#### *Romans 11:1-36*

Anti-Semitism:

##### Summary of Romans 9-11

God's Word to Israel has not failed. 

1. His decisions are beyond our scrutiny. (ch. 9)
2. People are saved by faith. (ch. 10)
3. He will restore Israel. (ch. 11)
